Privacy Policy for Alarm.Ai

Effective date: February 17, 2026

Alarm.Ai ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Alarm.Ai Android app (package name com.alarmai.alarm_ai).

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

  1. Information you provide in the app
    • Alarm settings and schedules
    • Alarm labels, routines, and related preferences
    • Optional contact information you choose to use inside app features
  2. Google account information (optional)
    • If you sign in with Google, we may access basic profile details such as your name, email address, and profile photo as provided by Google.
  3. Device and app data
    • Basic technical data needed to run app features, such as notification/alarm status and app configuration.
  4. Permissions we may request
    • POST_NOTIFICATIONS, SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM, WAKE_LOCK, RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED, and related alarm/device permissions to deliver alarms reliably.
    • READ_CONTACTS if you use contact-based features.
    • CAMERA if you use camera-based features.
    • ACCESS_NOTIFICATION_POLICY, PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS, and accessibility-related access only when you enable focus or interruption-control features.

How We Use Information

We use data to:

  • Run core alarm and reminder functionality
  • Support optional features you choose to enable
  • Maintain app reliability and performance
  • Provide account-related functionality when Google Sign-In is used

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share limited data only:

  • With service providers required for app functionality (for example, Google Sign-In / Google APIs)
  • If required by law, legal process, or to protect rights and safety

Data Storage and Retention

  • Most alarm and preference data is stored locally on your device.
  • Data remains until you delete it in the app or uninstall the app, unless a longer retention period is legally required.

Your Choices

  • You can deny permissions in Android settings. Some features may stop working.
  • You can remove app data by clearing storage or uninstalling the app.
  • You can manage Google account access through your Google account settings.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect information. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

Alarm.Ai is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted at the same URL with a new effective date.
